Site Purpose

Eco Green is a central resource for individuals interested in sustainable living. The site offers eco-tips, DIY green projects, recommended eco-friendly products, environmental news, and a blog for ongoing education and inspiration.

Scenarios

  • What are some beginner-friendly tips for reducing plastic use at home?
  • Where can I find verified eco-friendly brands or product suggestions?

Color Schema

  • Forest Green (#2d6a4f): Primary color for headers, buttons, and navigation.
  • Mint Green (#52b788): Accent color for highlights, links, and icons.

Typography

  • Merriweather: For headings and navigation. Elegant and strong for titles.
  • Open Sans: For body text. Clean and readable for general content and blog posts.

Desktop View

    • Main articles/blog section
