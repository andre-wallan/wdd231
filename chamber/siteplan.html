Site Plan: Eco Green

Site Name

Eco Green

This name reflects the website's mission to promote sustainable living, eco-friendly products, and green lifestyle education. Itâ€™s short, memorable, and directly aligned with the site's values.

Potential domain: ecogreenlife.org

Site Purpose

Eco Green serves as a central resource for individuals interested in living sustainably. The website will offer eco-tips, DIY green projects, recommended eco-friendly products, news on environmental issues, and a blog for ongoing education and inspiration.

Scenarios

"What are some beginner-friendly tips for reducing plastic use at home?"
"Where can I find verified eco-friendly brands or product suggestions?"

Color Schema

Primary Color - Forest Green (#2d6a4f): Used for headers, buttons, and navigation elements.

Accent Color - Mint Green (#52b788): Used for highlights, links, and icons to give a fresh, natural tone.

Typography

Merriweather

Used for headings and navigation elements. Elegant and strong for titles and major headings.

Open Sans

Used for body text and general content. Clean and readable for general content and blog posts.

Wireframes

Mobile View

  • Header with logo and menu icon
  • Hero image with welcome text and CTA
  • Sections stacked vertically:
    • Intro/About
    • Featured blog posts
    • Product highlights
    • Newsletter signup
  • Footer with contact links and socials

Desktop View

  • Header with full nav bar and logo
  • Full-width hero section with green-themed background and text overlay
  • Three-column layout:
    • Main articles/blog section
    • Sidebar with quick tips and recent posts
    • Product showcase or eco-news
  • Footer with sitemap and newsletter form
