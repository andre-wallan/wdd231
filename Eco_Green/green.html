Website Plan: Eco Green

Site Name

Eco Green

This name reflects the website's mission to promote sustainable living, eco-friendly products, and green lifestyle education. It’s short, memorable, and directly aligned with the site's values.

Optional domain: ecogreenlife.org

Site Purpose

The website serves as a central resource for individuals interested in living sustainably. It will offer eco-tips, DIY green projects, recommended eco-friendly products, news on environmental issues, and a blog for ongoing education and inspiration.

Scenarios

  • What are some beginner-friendly tips for reducing plastic use at home?
  • Where can I find verified eco-friendly brands or product suggestions?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color - #2d6a4f (Forest Green): Used for headers, buttons, and navigation elements.
  • Accent Color - #52b788 (Mint Green): Used for highlights, links, and icons to give a fresh, natural tone.

Typography

  • Headings: Merriweather – serif, elegant and strong for titles and major headings.
  • Body Text: Open Sans – sans-serif, clean and readable for general content and blog posts.

Wireframe

Mobile View (sketch)

  • Header with logo and menu icon
  • Hero image with welcome text and CTA
  • Sections stacked vertically:
    • Intro/About
    • Featured blog posts
    • Product highlights
    • Newsletter signup
  • Footer with contact links and socials

Desktop View (sketch)

  • Header with full nav bar and logo
  • Full-width hero section with green-themed background and text overlay
  • Three-column layout:
    • Main articles/blog section
    • Sidebar with quick tips and recent posts
    • Product showcase or eco-news
  • Footer with sitemap and newsletter form

Testing

  • HTML passes validation (W3C checker).
  • Good contrast between text and background for accessibility.
  • Responsive layout verified on mobile and desktop sizes.
  • Lighthouse checks passed for SEO, performance, and best practices.
